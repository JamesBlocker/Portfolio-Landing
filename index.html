My story so far...

I am deeply interested in the evolving role technology plays in human creativity and expression. Growing up a musician, I found myself experimenting with exciting new technologies as the whole industry begrudgingly realized the power that digital media offered over analog. Working as a full-time touring musician for over a decade I found myself taking on new roles, one being designing marketing material for several bands. This sparked an interest in graphic design so I went back to school and started working at a Clear Channel radio station in the art department. Again the whole industry saw paper advertisement dwindle as web advertising skyrocketed. I moved back into music and have been doing publishing and licensing for the last few years. I went back to school this time to learn Computer Programming because I am so excited by the way technology is moving the creative arts and I want to have a hand in shaping that for future creators!
